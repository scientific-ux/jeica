About Jeica

Founded by Wasswa Umar, Jeica Jewelry & Watches is more than brand-it's a movement. Born in Kampala and insipred by African royalty and modern street luxury, Jeica speaks to the bold, the stylish, and the unforgettable.

Our jewelry is designed to amplify confidence and showcase individuality. Whether you're on campus, stage, or making a statement in everyday life, Jeica is here to complete your look with timeless elegance and modern flair.

Our Mission

To inspire confidence and elevate personal style through high-quality, affordable jewelry and watches that celebrate individuality, creativity, and culture.

Our Vision

To become Uganda's most iconic jewelry and fashion-forward watch brand, empowering youth and creatives across Africa and beyond.

Our Values

  • Authenticity - We stay true to who we are and celebrate our roots.
  • Creativity - Bold, unique designs inspired by youth culture.
  • Quality - Durable, stylish, timeless pieces.
